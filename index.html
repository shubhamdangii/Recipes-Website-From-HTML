STARTER :

Rescipe Name Ingredients Instruction
Aloo
Tikki
  • Potatoes
  • Red chili powder
  • Garam masala
  • Salt
  • Oil
  1. Boil and mash 2-3 potatoes. Add 1/4 cup bread crumbs, 1 tsp grated ginger, 1 chopped green chili, 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves, 1/2 tsp red chili powder, 1/2 tsp garam masala, optional 1/2 tsp amchur powder, and salt.
  2. Shape the mixture into patties.
  3. Shallow fry the patties in oil until golden brown and crispy on both sides.
  4. Serve hot with chutney or yogurt.
Besan
Chilla
  • flour (besan)
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chili powder
  1. Mix besan (gram flour) with water to make a smooth batter.
  2. Add salt, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and any other desired spices.
  3. Heat a griddle or non-stick pan and spread a ladleful of batter evenly.
  4. Cook on medium heat until the bottom side turns golden brown.
  5. Flip the chilla and cook the other side until crispy. Serve hot with chutney or yogurt.

Main Course :

Rescipe Name Ingredients Instruction
Malai
Piyaz
  • Onions
  • Fresh coriander leaves (optional)
  • Chaat masala (optional)
  • Lemon juice
  • Black pepper
  • Cream (Malai)
  • Salt
  1. Slice onions.
  2. Mix onions with cream.
  3. Add salt, black pepper, and lemon juice.
  4. Optional: Sprinkle chaat masala.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
Paneer
Bhurji
  • Paneer (cottage cheese), crumbled
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Tomato, finely chopped
  • Green chili, finely chopped (optional)
  • Ginger, grated or finely chopped
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Salt
  • Oil or ghee for cooking
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)
  1. Heat oil or ghee in a pan on medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
  3. Add grated ginger and chopped green chili (if using). Sauté for a minute.
  4. Add the finely chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  5. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt to taste. Mix well.
  6. Add crumbled paneer to the pan and stir to combine with the spices.
  7. Cook for a few minutes, stirring occasionally, until the paneer is heated through.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  9. Serve hot with roti, naan, or rice.

DESERT :

Rescipe Name Ingredients Instruction
Shrikhand
  • Greek yogurt (hung curd)
  • Powdered sugar
  • Saffron strands (optional)
  • Cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts (such as almonds, pistachios) for garnish
  1. Take hung curd (Greek yogurt) in a bowl.
  2. Add powdered sugar, saffron strands (soaked in a little milk for color and flavor), and cardamom powder.
  3. Mix everything well until it becomes smooth and creamy.
  4. Garnish with chopped nuts.
  5. Serve chilled as a refreshing and creamy dessert.
Coconut
Ladoo
  • Desiccated coconut
  • Condensed milk
  1. In a bowl, mix desiccated coconut and condensed milk together.
  2. Shape the mixture into small round balls or ladoos.
  3. Let them cool in the refrigerator for a few hours until they become firm.
  4. Coconut ladoos are ready to be enjoyed as a simple and delicious dessert.
